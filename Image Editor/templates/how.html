How to Use Doc Scanner

1. Upload Your Documents:

Once logged in, you'll be directed to your Doc Scanner dashboard. To upload documents, click on the "Upload" button and select the files you wish to scan or manage from your device.

You can upload images, PDFs, or other supported document formats to begin processing.


2. Choose Your Processing Options:

After uploading your documents, you'll have various processing options available, depending on your requirements. These may include:

  • Image to PDF Conversion: Convert individual images or a series of images into PDF format for easy sharing and archiving.
  • Text Extraction: Extract text from scanned documents to make them searchable and editable.
  • Document Organization: Organize your files into folders or categories for efficient management.
  • Editing Tools: Edit, annotate, or watermark your documents as needed.

3. Review and Save Your Documents:

Once you've applied the desired processing options, review your documents to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Click on the "Save" or "Download" button to save your processed documents to your device or cloud storage. You may also choose to share them directly from the platform.


4. Explore Advanced Features (Optional):

Doc Scanner offers advanced features for users seeking additional functionality, such as:

  • Optical Character Recognition (OCR): Recognize text within images or scanned documents to enable text search and manipulation.
  • Batch Processing: Process multiple documents simultaneously to save time and effort.
  • Integration with Third-Party Services: Connect Doc Scanner with your favorite cloud storage platforms or productivity tools for seamless workflow integration.

5. Get Help and Support:

If you encounter any issues or have questions about using Doc Scanner, visit our "Help" section or contact our support team for assistance. We're here to help you make the most of our platform.

Start using Doc Scanner today and experience the convenience of efficient document scanning and management! If you have any feedback or suggestions for improving our platform, we'd love to hear from you. Thank you for choosing Doc Scanner!
